Play

How To Play?

To control the paddle, press the left or right arrow keys to move it left and right. To change the difficulty, press "2" for hard mode, press "3" for difficult mode, and press "1" to return to the normal and default mode.



About

Project Description

The objective of the game is to hit and destroy every single brick by using a paddle and a ball. The paddle is controlled by the player to bounce the ball back up, while also preventing the ball from falling below; if it has fallen off or the player didn't manage to bounce it back, the game will end.


The bricks need to be hit 3 times to be fully destroyed, and there 3 difficulties, namely normal, hard, and difficult. The only difference between each difficulty is the speed of the ball and the points you get each hit. In normal mode, each brick you hit gives you 10 points, then 20 points in hard mode, and lastly 30 points in difficult mode.
